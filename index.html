Biography

Our newly formed clarinet trio is made up of three musicians who share the aim to take their chamber music playing to the highest level. We share the strength of musical versatility, and this lies at the heart of our ambitions as a group. We aim to present diverse and eclectic programmes in traditional as well as unexpected venues, making efforts to programme music by female and minors composers alongside staple pieces of the trio repertoire. We formed the Farrenc trio out of our collective passion to explore and fuse the staple pieces in the clarinet trio oeuvre with contemporary repertoire, especially with the look to commissioning works on a regular basis. At this stage in our development, we are focusing on continually expanding our repertoire, recording ourselves on a regular basis, putting a diverse range of programmes together and organising regular performances. We currently have working relationships with concerts being planned with all of the following concert venues across London for concerts throughout 2022 including Holy Sepulchre Church; St George’s Church, Beckenham; St Matthew’s, Ealing; St Pancras Clock Tower; Fidelio Cafe and St John’s Waterloo to name a few. We will soon be recording pieces by Robert Muczynski, Louise Farrenc and Raymond Brien amongst others.
