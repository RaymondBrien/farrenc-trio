Contact Tips:

  • Please give us as many details as possible on what you are looking for. The more details about repertoire, travel arrangements, location and venue, event duration and dress code the better!
  • We provide quotes based on the information you give us. Should you have any questions, please don't hesitate to also refer to the Musicians Union rates guide.
  • We are based in London, UK and all hold UK citizenship. Please be aware that travel arrangements will need to be negotiated if your proposed event is outside of London.
  • Whether your event is a corporate event, recital (lunchtime or evening), we will be delighted to work with you.
